Selected Projects

Trained and tuned a YOLOv5 model to detect and classify 9 maritime classes with 0.86 mAP@0.5 for automating India's 7500km coastline.


Conversational AI Chatbot (Chubb Insurance) (project report)
Developed a coversational chatbot using a Neo4j Knowledge Graph, and engineered few-shot intent classification with SetFit.


POS Tagger for Hindi-English Code-Mixed Data (NLP Course Project) (code)
Developed a POS tagger tailored for code-mixed data, integrating NLTK for English and a trained HMM for Hindi into a unified tagging system.
